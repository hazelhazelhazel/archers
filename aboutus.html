Vision

ARCH aims to inspire social transformation within the Lasallian community through digital media production.

Mission

ARCH is committed to creating relevant and timely content that inspires transformative change while bringing entertainment to its audiences. With primarily Lasallian- produced content distributed across online media platforms, the organization is devoted to honing its members to be professional and creative media practitioners with integrity and ambition.
